        This is a scratch game, called Red Line Stop (RLS) that one 
tries to get the ball to stop on the line and if it is 
touching the red line you win with a total of 5 points. The 
ball sprites moves by itself. 
    
    In this program you click the CLICK TO START button to START the 
GAME and once the ball is on the red line or a little before
the CLICK TO STOP to ENDS the GAME and if the ball is touching the 
red line you win 5 and get five points for that game.
